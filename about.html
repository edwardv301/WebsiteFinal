About Social Media


Social Media is a term used to desrcibe a platform used for sharing information and ideas with others. Sites such as the instagram and twittter are social media platforms and are used by hundreds of millions of people yet they are all used in different ways.

For example, instagram is mostly used for photo sharing while twitter is used for connecting with others through their 'Tweets'. Social media also has a wide range of advantages and disadavantages when being used such as it being very addictive to many people.
